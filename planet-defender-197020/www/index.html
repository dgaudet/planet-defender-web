Planet Defender

An exciting game where you defend planets with nothing but your laser cannon.

  • First Line of Defence

    • Imagine that you have to defend the Universe.
    • The last weapon left to defend it is a satelite.
    • Luckily this satilite happens to have a laser cannon on board.

  • Save the Universe

    • The attack doesn't stop at Earth.
    • You must travel to far away planets.
    • So you can defend them as well.
    • You will have to blast through different enemies.
    • From comets, to rockets to aliens.
    • Do you have what it takes to save the Universe?
